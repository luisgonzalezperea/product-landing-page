The Black Phone

2021 | Horror/Thriller | 1h 42min

Sinopsis

Finney Shaw, a shy, intelligent 13-year-old boy, is kidnapped by a sadistic killer and trapped in a soundproof basement where screaming is useless. When a disconnected phone in the wall starts ringing, Finney discovers he can hear the voices of the killer's previous victims, who are determined to make sure that what happened to them doesn't happen to Finney.
